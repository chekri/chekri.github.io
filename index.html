About Me

I am a post-doctoral researcher in the University of Trento, Italy working in colloboration with Prof. Francesco Dal Corso and Prof. Davide Bigoni. I like modeling the materials and analyzing them with mathematical techniques, especially instabilities. There has been a surging interest in harnessing the material instabilities for applications and my research is handy in those circumstances.

Research

Interests: Calculus of Variations, Solid Mechanics, Nonlinear Dynamics, Optimization and Numerical Methods

Current Research: The dynamics of slender structures/metastructures

PhD research: 3D slender structures and instabilities
